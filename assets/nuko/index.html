Nuko is a small white cat character featured in mobile wallpapers, email decorations (decomail), and pixel-style graphics originally distributed through a Japanese subscription content service.

Where Did Nuko Come From?

Nuko appeared in downloadable content provided through a Japanese mobile service. These included wallpapers, stamps, animated GIFs, and decorative email graphics designed for flip phones and early smartphones.

Is Nuko the Same as Other White Cat Characters?

Although Nuko resembles other simple white cat mascots, (e.g 井上トロ) it is considered a separate character with its own content history.

Why Is It Popular?

Nuko’s simple pixel design, expressive poses, and nostalgic early-2000s mobile aesthetic make it especially appealing to fans of retro web culture and Japanese phone graphics.
